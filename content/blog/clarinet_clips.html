Tchaikovsky’s Nutcracker for the clarinet and orchestra

His playing is so lyrical. He sings with the clarinet. I can listen to his playing all day.

Spohr #2

He effortlessly plays this notoriously difficult piece. His clarinet produces a thin, light sound that is characteristic of the German clarinet.
